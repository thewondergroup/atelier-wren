Caia

Notting Hill, Londonwww.caia.com@caialondon

Chic and fun, with food, drink and music at its heart

The founders of Caia wanted to open a restaurant with food, drink and music at its heart. In equal measures chic and fun, the restaurant now proudly sits at the top of fashionable Meard Street in Notting Hill.

Since opening, Caia has won plaudits for its welcoming yet sophisticated design that features an open woodfire grill framed by a white textured-tiled and Nero marquina bar front and blackened steel gantry.

Downstairs diners and revellers are treated to DJs spinning tunes from a sapele DJ booth inspired by 70s home cocktail bars. Glistening crystal palm shaped wall-lights and Ralph Lauren fabric upholstered banquettes catch the eye.

A space where the warmth of natural materials meets the energy of live music — designed to make every guest feel like they've discovered somewhere truly special.

A design that celebrates craft, texture and atmosphere

Every surface has been considered — from the hand-laid tiles to the bespoke brass lighting fixtures, each element contributes to a space that feels both intimate and theatrical.
