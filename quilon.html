A refined reimagining of a Michelin-starred institution

A refined reimagining of a Michelin-starred institution, rooted in the traditions of India's southwest coast.

Atelier Wren draws on Kerala craftsmanship and coastal architecture, layering terracotta, timber and hand-worked details to create a space that feels both grounded and transportive.

Tour Quilon

From palm-frond motifs to antique doors and bronze detailing, each element reflects a deep respect for regional materiality and storytelling

The result is an immersive, atmospheric interior that balances authenticity with a contemporary sense of quiet luxury.
