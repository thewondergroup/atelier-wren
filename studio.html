Studio

Co-founded in 2021 by luxury hospitality designers Jake Raslan and Lorenzo Buscaroli

and boasting a truly multi-skilled team, Atelier Wren caters to all aspects of interior design.

Their ethos is that design should be both thoughtful and refined — but always imbued with a sense of joy and a creative edge. Atelier Wren's lighting design concepts are all developed in-house, ensuring a heightened sense of drama and excitement to any space.

"Atelier Wren are incredible people — timely, professional, respectful of budget as well as everyone on the job. I remain so impressed with their resourceful nature, creative openness & enormous talent."
